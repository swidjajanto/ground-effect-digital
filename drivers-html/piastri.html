Biography
From Melbourne to the front of the grid
Date of Birth 06 / 04 / 2001
Place of Birth Melbourne, Victoria, Australia

Growing up just a short distance from Albert Park, Oscar Piastri’s first taste of Formula 1 came from watching the world’s best race around his hometown streets. That early exposure turned into a focused push through karting and junior single-seater categories.

After success in Australia, Piastri moved to Europe to test himself against the strongest fields. He quickly built a reputation for consistency and speed, winning titles in Formula Renault, Formula 3 and Formula 2 on his way up the ladder.

His arrival in Formula 1 with McLaren confirmed the hype. Calm on the radio but ruthless on track, Piastri has blended precision, tyre management and racecraft to become one of the most exciting drivers of his generation.
