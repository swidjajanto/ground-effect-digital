Team Profile
Innovation, papaya, and a comeback story

From Bruce McLaren’s original vision to the papaya-orange machines of the modern era, McLaren has been synonymous with innovation and fearless racing. The team’s history is filled with legendary names – Senna, Prost, Hakkinen, Hamilton – and iconic cars that reset the standards of performance and design.

After a challenging period in the hybrid era, McLaren’s recent resurgence has been built on smarter aerodynamics, sharp strategy, and a young driver pairing capable of extracting the maximum from every upgrade package.

The current MCL39 continues that trajectory, trading drag for downforce in all the right places and turning McLaren into a constant podium threat – and increasingly, a favourite for race wins.
